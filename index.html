EXACT BEATS TABLE
King (♚) beats Queen, Jack — loses to Ace (Ace counters King!)
Queen (♛) beats Jack, Ace — loses to King
Jack (♞) beats Ace — loses to King, Queen
Ace (✦) beats King ONLY — loses to Queen, Jack; Ace vs Ace = −200 each
Same card → both return, no damage
ATTRITION & RESERVE
Win a clash → your card returns. Enemy card dumped, enemy reserve −1.
Lose a clash → your card dumped forever. Your reserve −1, hand refills.
Ace vs Ace → both dumped, both −200 HP, both reserves fill hands.
Same card draw → both return, no damage.
PASS → passer −50 HP. Both cards return.
MANA
Starts at 5. Gains +3 each round, capped at 10. Accumulates — never resets.
